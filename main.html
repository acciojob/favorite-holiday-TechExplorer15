Goa


Goa is a popular holiday destination known for its beautiful beaches and relaxed atmosphere.

  1. Baga Beach – famous for its nightlife
  2. Calangute Beach – known as the Queen of Beaches
  3. Basilica of Bom Jesus – a historic church
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – a natural attraction
  5. Anjuna Beach – popular for its flea market
  • Goan Fish Curry – a spicy dish
  • Prawn Balchão – a seafood delicacy
  • Bebinca – a traditional dessert
  • Pork Vindaloo – rich in spices
  • Feni – a local drink
